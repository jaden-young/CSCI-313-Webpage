Level 1 - Racer

Music, sounds, and voices

Go kart engine noise, various noises when the Go Kart collides with other objects in the world. Potential noises for acquisition of power ups. Background music will be behind the entire level. Victory sequence music and “Game Over” screen music. When the timer reaches certain time intervals there will be audio cues.
