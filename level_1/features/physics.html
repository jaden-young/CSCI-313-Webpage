Level 1 - Racer

Environmental forces and their affects

The Go Kart will be racing across various landscapes. These landscapes typically consisting of either dirt road or asphalt. Whenever the Go Kart collides with an object, the object will create a negative force on the Go Kart and slow down the Go Kart. Depending on the size of the object, this will determine the absolute affect of the objects on the Go Kart.

For example, if the object is the size of a building, the opposite force from it would be enough to cause the Go Kart to come to a complete stop. If it’s a smaller object like a cardboard box filled with kittens, the Go Kart would only be slightly slowed down.

Also, driving on surfaces that aren’t the main surface that the player should drive on will have a negative effect on the maximum speed of the Go Kart.
