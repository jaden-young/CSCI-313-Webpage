Level 1 - Racer

Structures, collectibles, and projectiles

Objects in this game will vary a considerable amount in terms of what they do and how they affect the game. Structures will be the most common type of object. The structures will serve as obstacles during the race and also as scenery. So there will be bigger objects like walls and buildings and smaller ones like bushes and puddles all with varying effects on the gameplay.

Collectibles will come in the form of coins (bottles of ranch) and will add time on clock. Throughout the course there will be a few areas that contain speed boosts. Getting to these areas will be more difficult so determining if they are worth it is up to the player.
