Level 1 - Racer

Game layout and map

This game will be played as a timed slalom race. There will be one racetrack that will have multiple race surfaces and many obstacles on and around the course. There are no laps and the objective is to get from point A to point B as fast as possible. The obstacles in the race will be implemented to make the race more challenging and fun for the player.
