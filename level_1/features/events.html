Level 1 - Racer

Things that trigger actions

Triggering the movement of the Go Kart by the user will cause the Go Kart to begin movement across the landscape. Crashing the Go Kart three times will cause a game over sequence for the level. Upon completion of the course, the player will be greeted with textboxes and a scene transition to the next level of the game.

Owner: Jordan Falcon
