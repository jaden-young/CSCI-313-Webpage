Level 1 - Racer

Avatar

Wagon / Cart

Methods

draw()

Draws the avatar.

reset()

The reset method resets the avatar to the beginning of the course if the racer loses a life or the timer runs out.

update()

Level Builder calls this when the Avatar collides with objects on the course.and also calls upon this to update movements throughout the race.

score()

The score method updates the score for the player when ranch bottles are collected and also once the player completes the course.

initialize()

The initialize method will initialize the Avatar and gather all of the necessary information that the avatar needs.
