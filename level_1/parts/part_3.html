Level 1 - Racer

Objects

Objects that the Avatar interacts with throughout the level

Methods

draw()

Draws the objects onto the screen.

update()

Level Builder uses this to update the objects when the Avatar collides with them.

collides()

Method that is called when the avatar collides with one of the objects (not including the Ranch Bottles) and causes the player to stop momentarily and makes the object disappear.

itemEffect()

Method that effects the Avatar when the Avatar collides with the Ranch Bottle that adds time to the timer and also adds points to the score. Calls upon the score() method in the Level Builder to update the Player's score.

initialize()

The initialize method will initialize the objects for the level.
