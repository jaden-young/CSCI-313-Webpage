Level 1 - Racer

Level Builder

Course, Structures, and Additional features (Arrow Spots, siding of track, & Puddles) listed on the track; Also initializes the Timer

Methods

draw()

Draws the track, structures, and additional features on the track.

update()

Level Builder uses this to constantly update the course as the player moves across the course.

boost()

Method that is called when the avatar collides with the spot on the course and causes the avatar to increase speed for three seconds.

initialize()

The initialize method will initialize the arrow spots and other scenery and also show the beginning screen showing the player instructions.

timer()

Method that initializes the timer and keeps track of said time throughout the level.

winLevel()

Method that is called once the player completes the course and finishes the level. Prompts the user to transition onto the next level.

loseLevel()

Method that is called when the player loses (loses a life or doesn't complete the course in time [timer runs out]) and resets the course and shows a screen informing the player that they have failed to complete the course.
