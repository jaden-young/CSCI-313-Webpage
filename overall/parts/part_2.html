Buttons

There are 6 buttons in totals (Racer, Side Scroller, Battle, MineSweeper, Simon, and Boss) each button is invisible to the user other than the text on the button.

The only thing called during these buttons is on the Canvas. When the button is clicked on, the object will send a number corresponding to the order it is in in the build sequence of the scen it wants to build.
