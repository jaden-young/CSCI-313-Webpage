Overall Story

Once upon a time, in the kingdom of Ranchdom, a knight awoke at the enterance of a castle. He remembered nothing about his life before waking up. He was trying to decide what to do when a mysterious voice called out him tellimg him to make his way though the castle. That the voice needed the brave knight's help to get free from the castle. He warned the knight that it would not be easy as there were many challenges in the castle he would need to defeat. The knight decided to help this mysterious voice and enter the first part of the castle.
