Rules

  • A player is given 3 lives to start the game. They lose a life by dying during that level.
  • Once a player loses all of their lives, they must start over at first level.
  • A player will gain points as he goes through each level and they will collect into a total score either when the player dies or when the player wins the game.
  • A player can collect collectibles (ranch bottles) during each level to be used as a multipler at the end of each level.
  • Once a player completes a level, another life is added to the amount of lives they have.
  • Each level decided how the player will lose a life.

Objectives

  • The object is to complete each level and defeat the boss at the end
