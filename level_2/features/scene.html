Level 2 - Side Scroller

Game layout and map

The above image will be the map layout for the side scroller. There is a key to describe some of the objects. The player will start on the far left and work their way right. Once they reach the door, they will enter and move on to the next level, which is the Battle. All of the rectangles will be made out of brick, and a darker color to them. This way we keep with the castle theme.

As you can see, the player will be required to do a variety of jumps, such as wall jump and double jumps. Keep in mind there is room for possible variation in length of the level down the road (make it longer or shorter). As well as change in structures (may or may not have time to do falling structures).
