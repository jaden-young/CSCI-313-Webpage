EndGame

Player Point of View
If Avatar collides with the Door at the end of the game. They will enter and then a congratulations window will pop up indicating the game is done also displaying the score.
Developer Point of View
The Avatar will have to check for collisions with the Door. Once the have collided with the door, they will the indicate to the LevelManage that the game is complete. This will display the score and allow the player to move to the next level.
