Level 2 - Side Scroller

Player Gets Hit

    Developer POV

  1. If avatar has a power up, the avatar will lose said powerup, avatar becomes invulnerable for 2 seconds
  2. If avatar has no power up and gets hit, the avatar dies
  3. Plays hit sound

Player POV

The avatar gets hits by an enemy. If there is a current PowerUp effect, the avatar will lose the PowerUp and start to fade in and out for 2 seconds. If there is not a PowerUp, the avatar will die(showing death effect/sound). Then he will restart the level at the starting point.
