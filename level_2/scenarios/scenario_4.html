Level 2 - Side Scroller

Player Hits Enemy

    Developer POV

  1. Enemy dies
  2. Enemy plays death animation
  3. Enemy plays death sound
  4. Player gains score accordingly

    5. Player POV

  5. If the player jumps on top of the enemies head, the enemy will die and the player will gain score.
  6. If the player attacks an enemy head on with a weapon, the enemy will die and the players will gain score.
  7. When the enemy dies, it will play a death animation along with a death sound.
