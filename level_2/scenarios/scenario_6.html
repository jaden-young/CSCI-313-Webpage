Colliding with Floating Structures

Player Point of View
If Avatar jumps into a floating structure.They will stop in place and fall straight down. If he jumps into a floating structure while pressing down on side arrow keys, he will then fly off in the opposite direciton (a.k.a. Wall Jump).
Developer Point of View
The Avatar will have to check for collisions with Floating Structures. In the collision check they will have to also check for buttons pressed.
