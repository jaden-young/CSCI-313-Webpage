Level 5 - Boss Battle

Player walks through fireball's impact area while it is on fire

  1. The player's health is reduced by a small amount of damage over time from burning
  2. Burning sound while damage over time is active
  3. A small amount of embers and smoke appear in view as if they are on the player

Developer's Perspective

On a collision between the player avatar and the FireAOE objects damageRadius, the FireAOE will pass data to the avatar's takeDamage method, including total damage, duration, and rate at which to apply. On a collision the FireAOE will also play a burning sound, and the system will call a method "burn" to show burning embers and smoke on the camera.
