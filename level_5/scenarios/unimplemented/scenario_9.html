Level 5 - Boss Battle

Fireball hits the ground

  1. Sends out a tremor in a small radius around the fireball
  2. Small explosion on ground where fireball hits
  3. Briefly sets the area hit by the fireball on fire

Developer's Perspective

When the Dragon calls its breathFire method, it creates a fireball object with properties for size and trajectory. The Fireball object's update method checks for a collision with the ground. On a collision, the Fireball creates a FireballAOE object that sets the collision are on fire for a set ammount of time.
