Level 5 - Boss Battle

Player does not take damage for 8 seconds

  1. Player will slowly regenerate health until back to full health

Developer's Perspective

A private "heal" method will be called during the player avatar's update method. This will check for the last time the player was damaged, and if 8 second have elapsed since that time the player will begin to regenerate health.
