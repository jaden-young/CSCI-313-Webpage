Level 5 - Boss Battle

Player is in fireball's tremor radius

  1. Player's screen shakes once

Developer's Perspective

On the frame that the FireAOE object is created, the system checks for a collision between the FireAOE termor radius and the Avatar. If they do collide, the system will cause the camera to shake.
