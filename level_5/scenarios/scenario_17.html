Level 5 - Boss Battle

Player Hit by Dragon Attack

  1. Player is hit by dragon's attack
  2. Player's health bar decreases

Developer's Perspective

Again, we make use of Unity's tag and message system. When the player is hit by an attack from the Dragon, its OnTriggerEnter method is called. The dragon then uses the SendMessage method to tell our player's PlayerHealth script to take the specified amount of damage. If the player's shield has told the PlayerHealth script that we are currently blocking, that damage is to be reduced by half. The PlayerHealth script then updates the slider UI element representing the player's health.
