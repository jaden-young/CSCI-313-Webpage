Level 5 - Boss Battle

Player walks through the gate and opens the chest

  1. Animation of the chest opening and a bottle of ranch slowly rises from within
  2. Chest opening and ranch rising sound effects play
  3. Victory music starts
  4. Final score screen is displayed

Developer's Perspective

This scenario is triggered by the box collider marked as a trigger attached to the Chest in its OnTriggerEnter method. The ChestController script then calls its open method, as well as the BottleOfRanchController's RaiseBottle method. When the bottle of ranch is done rising out of the chest, the LevelFiveManager script will return the player to the main menu.
