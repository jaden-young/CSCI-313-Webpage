Level 5 - Boss Battle

Player Enters Level

Oiriginal

  1. Camera pans from first person view to a floating camera view that rapidly zooms in on a dragon.
  2. Dragon roars and shakes its head from side to side while spitting fire
  3. Dragon settles into starting battle position with smoke coming its nostrils
  4. Camera zooms out back into the player's first person view
  5. Battle Music starts on a loop

Developer's Perspective

Actual

The only thing we implemented to happen upon player entry is having the dragon roar.

Developer's Perspective

Make use of the Dragon's Start method, which is called when the dragon enters the game, i.e. at the very beginning of the level. The dragon stops chasing the player, and plays its roar animation and sound effect.
