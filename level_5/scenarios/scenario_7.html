Level 5 - Boss Battle

Player's health gets reduced to 0 or less

Original

  1. Battle music ends
  2. Death animation and camera pans out to see the player's body laying on the ground and the dragon shaking it's head, spitting fire and ending with the dragon roaring
  3. Player loses a life
  4. If player has a remaining life, they start the level over Dragon back to full health Scenario 1 occurs again
  5. If player has no lives remaining they are shown a game over screen and returned to the main menu

Developer's Perspective

Actual

Since we went with an arcade style game instead of our game being a linear progression, we opted against having lives. Instead, the player is simply returned to the main menu upon death.

  1. Player death sound effect plays
  2. Screen goes red
  3. Player is returned to main menu

Developer's Perspective

Dealing damage to the player is handled by the TakeDamage method located in the player (avatar) object. When the player reaches 0 health, the LevelFiveManager script will end the game.
