Level 5 - Boss Battle

Player gets in attack range

  1. Player can use sword to deal damage to the dragon
  2. Dragon can blow person back using its wings
  3. Dragon can swing its tail at the player
  4. Dragon will do the following
    1. If player is not in front of the dragon, the dragon will rotate towards the player.
    2. If player is within claw range, the dragon will perform a flutter kick attack.
    3. If player is in headswipe range, the dragon will perform a head swipe attack.

Original Developer's Perspective

Actual Developer's Perspective

During the dragon's Update method, the dragon will get a reference to the player's current position. It will then calculate the angle between the player and the dragon. It will use this information to choose an action to perform from number 4 above.
