Level 5 - Boss Battle

Player Raises Shield

  1. Player hold right mouse button
  2. Player's shield is raised

Developer's Perspective

The PlayerDefend script located in player's shield will play its raise animation. This animation has animation events set in it to set the isBlocking boolean variable of the PlayerHealth script attached to the parent Player object. Whether the player is blocking or not affects how much damage they take
