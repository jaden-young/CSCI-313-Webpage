Level 5 - Boss Battle

Player moves around in the level

Original

  1. The dragon's head tracks the player
  2. The dragon slowly walks toward the player's position, but stays within a designated radius of its original position
  3. A fireball gets shot from the dragon's mouth periodically at the player

Developer's Perspective

Actual

We don't have the dragon's head tracking the player, instead the dragon's entire body follows the player as it moves. We also removed the restriction on having the dragon stay close to the gate, it will instead follow the player all over the arena. There is no escape from Terry the Terrible.

Developer's Perspective

Player is controlled by the FirstPersonController script (standard asset) attached to the player. Uses standard controls, wasd, shift for sprint, space for jump.

Dragon moving towards the player is handled by the Chase method in the DragonAI script, using its NavMeshAgent. This is used by referencing the position of the player and setting that position as the destination of the agent.
