Level 5 - Boss Battle

Dragon's health is reduced to 0 or less

  1. Dragon's death animation starts Dragon roars then falls over dead
  2. Dragon's body remains on the ground Falls in a designated position so as to not fall on top of the player
  3. After the dragon's death animation, camera switches to the gate behind where the dragon was and watches the animation of the gate opening
  4. Gate opens and sound effect plays, camera stays on first person player.

Developer's Perspective

The Dragon's death method causes the dragon to cease chasing the player and play a death animation and sound effect. The DragonAI script then calls the Gate's open method to open the gate, playing appropriate animation and sound effect.
