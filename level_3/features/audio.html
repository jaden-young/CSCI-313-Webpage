Level 3 - Battle!

Music, sounds, and voices

We will use music from games such as World of Warcraft in order to create a medieval battle atmosphere.

For the skeletons there will be the sounds of rambling bones. For the wraiths we will use a whooshing sound when they attack.
For the dread knights we will use a clanking sound for when they hit the player, this is also for the skeletons.
For the lich, there will be a zap sound effect for his attack. There will also be generic footstep sounds for the player.

For voices, we will just have the player speak in text boxes.
