Level 3 - Battle!

Avatars, and NPCs

Our battle segment will consist of 10 waves of enemies. In the first few waves there will be skeletons. These are the weakest of the enemies.

After wave 3, wraiths will start spawning, which are stronger than skeletons and can move through objects.

After wave 5, Dread Knights will start spawning, which are the fastest enemies and can deal a lot of damage.

After wave 7, liches will start spawning, which are ranged and have an area of effect attack.
