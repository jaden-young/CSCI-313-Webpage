Level 3 - Battle!

Game layout and map

Our level will be a rectangular arena in which monster spawners will be in the 4 corners of the level and the monsters will come towards where the player is. There will be random obstacles scattered throughout the level. Note: the view of the arena will be at an angled top down perspective.
