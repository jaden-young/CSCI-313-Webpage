Level 3 - Battle!

Structures, collectibles, and projectiles

There will be bottles of ranch that will be randomly dropped by slain enemies that will restore a portion of your health. There will also be mana potions scattered throughout the level. In addition, there will be powerups that will enhance the player’s abilities in various ways.

For structures, there will be randomly placed boulders and piles of rubble throughout the map for the player to utilize. There are no projectiles except for the lich’s area of effect blue energy attack. Below is the blade the wraith will use, and all other weapons are included in the illustrations of our enemy npc’s.
