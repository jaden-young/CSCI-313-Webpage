Level 3-Battle

Player Dies

Player's Presepective

The player has had their health bar depleted through being attacked by enemies and has died.

Through this, the live count on the top of the screen is decreased by one and the player despawns and
respawns back at the center of the arena at the beginning of the current wave. If all lives are lost, the player is sent to level 1 of the game.
