Methods

hit():

The player avatar swings sword and makes contact with enemy unit.

castDefensiveSpell():

Player immune to losses in health, but only when mana available (decreases over time, 5 mana per second of 100 used).

castOffensiveSpell():

With sword extended, spins avatar to hit enemies in short radius 50 damage (10 second cooldown, 25 mana out of 100 used).

dies():

Reduces life count by one (called when health goes to zero).
