Properties

Lives

Methods

moveAvatar():

Moves avatar to location of cursor right click on screen, stops avatar if encounters obstacle.

moveEnemy():

Enemy movement is part of A.I., if obstacle in the way, moves around it and always pursues avatar.

spawnPowerUp():

Randomly spawns a random selection of power up for the player to pick up at various points in game.

despawnPowerUp():

Despawns power up if out for 15 seconds.

exitLevel():

If lives property is labeled as zero (player dies() method is tracked 3 times) then exit level and start entire game over.

exitWave():

Any deaths before 3 result in player going back to wave one of current game.

initialize():

Initializes arena and avatar.

initialDraw():

Draws arena and avatar.

spawnEnemy():

Randomly spawns a random selection of enemies (varies by wave).
