Level 3-Battle

Enemy Units

Properties

Health

Methods

hit():

  • If Lich, swings sword to hit player causing 30 health points to be lost (out of 100).
  • If Wraith, swings sword to hit player causing 15 health points to be lost (out of 100).
  • If Skeleton, swings sword to hit player causing 10 health points to be lost (out of 100).
  • If Dread Knight, swings sword to hit player causing 23 health points to be lost (out of 100).

dies():

If health on unit drains to zero, they are elmininated and disappear from game.
