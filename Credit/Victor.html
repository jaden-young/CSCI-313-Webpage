I created the Enemy object. I used a skeleton from the unity asset store and used the dark skeleton model.
and made it able to follow the player.

I also used the Avatar object. I used animations that Weston had configured in order to correspond with the player attacking an enemy and the attack removing health from the enemy.

I also configured the enemy AI to attack and follow the player, along with balancing damage values on the enemy and player attacks in order to make for a fair gameplay experience. I also configured the skeleton's animations to work with various actions such as attacking, taking damage, etc.

In addition to all of things stated above I also worked alongside with Weston on making the Q spell work. I was particularly in charge of making the Q spell damage the enemies and trigger their animations.
