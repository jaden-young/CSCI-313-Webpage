I created the player avatar object. I ended up using a knight model from the unity asset store and modified it to have it function
and move via clicking to move as well as programming the keybinds for attacking and spellcasting.
As another note - I configured the avatar's health point and mana point trackers and their regenerative properties.

I also created the power up objects. I created spheres and colored them red, blue and white in correspondence with their effects (health, mana, then health and mana respectively).
Also placed them at random spots on the map.

Also created the arena, and was in charge of placement of various textured rock objects.

Finally I was in charge of implementing the user interface and all its instruction along with the addition of background music, which was a sound file from another game entitled "Kingdom Hearts".

To note: I performed maintenance on a variety of already completed scripts, mainly with enemy health management, physics, as well as coordinating and linking the enemy script to our player script.
