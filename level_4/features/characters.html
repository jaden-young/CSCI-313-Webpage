Level 4 - Puzzle

Avatars, and NPCs

The only player in this level is the main character. The main character is the avatar "knight" that has been used throughout all of the levels. Scores and number of lives will be attached to the player from previous levels, and depending on whether the player has completed/or lost level 4, the number of scores and lives will be increased or decreeased.
