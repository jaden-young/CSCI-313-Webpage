Level 4 - Puzzle

Environmental forces and their effects

There will be physics involved within the maze itself. The maze's walls will have to be enclosed to make sure that the avatar doesn't go underneath the maze walls and the maze floor. The system will also be checking for collisions between the avatar and the maze walls/puzzle doors.

The physics within the rest of the level will be minimal because when the game shifts from a 3D maze to a 2D puzzle, there isn't much physics involved when the mouse clicks initiate any kind of action/event.
