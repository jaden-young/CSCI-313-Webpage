Level 4 - Puzzle

Structures, collectibles, and projectiles

The objects in this level are the maze, avatar, and the puzzles.

The maze also will have Puzzle Door which will be used to activate the puzzle to start.

The memory game will contain cards that are flipped and compared to another card to see if they match.

The simon says game will contain a board and pictures. the pictures displayed on the screen for the user to click on to repeat the pattern. The board will show the pattern, create the pattern and see if the clicked on picture is correct.
