Level 4 - Puzzle

Game layout and map

You enter a hedged maze and have to find your way to the first door. At the first door, the player must complete a game of memory with making less than three mistakes to continiue. After completing memory, the player will have to find their way to the second puzzle. They must complete a game of Simon Says to complete the puzzle and unlock the door. Than the user need to find their way to the exit to finish the level.

Owner: Kelsey Bedard
