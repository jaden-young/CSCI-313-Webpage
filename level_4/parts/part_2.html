Level 4-Puzzle

Maze

Model

Methods

draw()

The draw method draws the maze.

collides()

The collides method will determine if the avatar has collided with the maze.

hide()

The hide method hides the maze when the avatar collides with the puzzle door indicating that the puzzle has started.

delete()

The delete method will delete the maze when the player has finished the level.

initialize()

The initialize method will initialize the maze.
