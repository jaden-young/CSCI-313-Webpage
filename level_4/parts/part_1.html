Level 4-Puzzle

Avatar

Model

Methods

draw()

The draw method draws the avatar.

reset()

The reset method resets the avatar to the beginning of the maze when the avatar loses a life after the first puzzle.

update()

The Level Builder will call the update method which would detect collisions and would not allow the avatar to move in that direction any farther.

hide()

The hide method hides the avatar when the avatar collides with the puzzle door.

updateScore()

The update score method updates the score for the player after a completion of a puzzle.

initialize()

The initialize method will initialize the Avatar and gather all of the necessary information that the avatar needs.
