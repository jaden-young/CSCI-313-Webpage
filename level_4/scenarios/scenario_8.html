Level 4-Puzzle

Player loses Memory

Player's Presepective

The player will have just flipped a card and lost his/her last try

Developer's Perspective

The Memory Manager will be notified that this was the last try and that the player lost. The Memory Manager will notify the Level Builder that the game was lost. The level builder will tell the avatar that it lost a life and it will have it reset at the beginning of the maze. It will also tell the maze to appear.
