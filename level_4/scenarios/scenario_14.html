Level 4-Puzzle

Player Loses Simon Says Game

Player's Presepective

The player has lost all his/her tries to complete Simon Says

Developer's Perspective

The Simon Says manager will be notified that this was the last try and that the player lost. The Simon Says Manager will notify the Level Builder that the game was lost. The Level Builder will tell the avatar that it lost a life and have it reset at the open door of the memory game. It will also tell the maze to appear.
