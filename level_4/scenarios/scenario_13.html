Level 4-Puzzle

Player Wins Simon Says Game

Player's Presepective

The play has correctly completed the last sequence of Simon

Developer's Perspective

Simon Says Manager will be notified that this was the last sequence was correct. It will find the total score of the puzzle by multiplying the tries left by the score received during the puzzle. It will than give that info to the Level builder to increase the overall score for the player. Simon Says Manager will than pass control back to Level Builder which will delete the Simon Says manager. It will make the maze and avatar visible again and allow the player to continiue on in the maze.
