Level 4-Puzzle

Player Wins Minesweeper Game

Player's Perspective

The player clicks on all of the tiles in the grid that don't have mines underneath it.

Developer's Perspective

The tile will uncover itself and all nearby tiles without mines. Once all of the non-mined tiles on the grid have been uncovered, the grid script will end the game and the player can choose to restart the game or return to the main menu.
