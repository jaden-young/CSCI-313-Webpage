Level 4-Puzzle

Simon Says Starts

Player's Presepective

The player will have just finished colliding with the Puzzle Door. The player now see the board for the Simon Says puzzle. Instructions will pop up.

Developer's Perspective

The Simon Says Manager will have just been initialized. It will then initialize the Board. The board will initialize and draw the Picture (4 of them). The board will generate a random sequence of the 4 pictures and display that sequence to the user.
