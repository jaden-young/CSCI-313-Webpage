Level 4-Puzzle

Player Enters Level 4

Player's Presepective

The player will have just finished level 3 and be starting level 4.

Developer's Perspective

The Game Manager (also known as the Scene Builder, which changes control between levels), will initialize the Level Builder. The level builder will display instructions for the player to know how to complete the level. Then the level builder will initialize and draw both the Avatar and Maze.
