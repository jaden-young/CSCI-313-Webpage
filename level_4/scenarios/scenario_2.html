Level 4-Puzzle

Player Moves in Maze

Player's Presepective

The player will be moving in the maze and possibly colliding with the maze wall. This scenario will deal with the player colliding with the maze wall.

Developer's Perspective

The Level builder will be calling the avatar's update method which is checking for collisions. The update method will detect the collision and not allow the player to move in that direction any farther.
