Level 4-Puzzle

Player Clicks On a Tile With No Mine

Player's Perspective

The player clicks on a random tile and either adjacent tiles are uncovered or none are.

Developer's Perspective

The tile will uncover and if there are no mines underneath it, nearby tiles will uncover too. Then the tile calls on the update method in the Grid script and that will update the grid and check for the number of mines still available.
